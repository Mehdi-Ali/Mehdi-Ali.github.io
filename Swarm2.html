SWARM 2 (2024)

VR roguelike arcade shooter

Project Description

SWARM 2 is an electrifying roguelike shooter that builds upon the fast-paced, high-flying action of its predecessor. Armed with a grappling hook and dual pistols, players swing through neon-lit battlegrounds, battling relentless waves of enemies while adapting to unpredictable challenges. With deep roguelike progression, globally competitive leaderboards, and a refined grappling system, SWARM 2 delivers an intense and immersive VR experience.

Every run presents unique encounters, forcing players to refine their skills, experiment with powerful upgrades, and master the momentum-based movement system. With each battle, they grow stronger, uncover hidden secrets, and fight to reclaim Earth from the Swarm.
