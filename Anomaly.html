Anomaly (2025)

Open World Multiplayer PvPvE VR Extraction Shooter

Project Description

Anomaly is a high-stakes PvPvE extraction shooter built for VR, where up to 12 players fight through a disaster-stricken city, battle unearthly creatures, and outsmart rival squads to secure valuable loot before extracting. With deep weapon customization, strategic decision-making, and intense multiplayer encounters, Anomaly delivers an immersive and adrenaline-fueled experience.

Developing Anomaly involved overcoming unique challenges in VR optimization, networking, and AI, ensuring smooth performance on mobile VR hardware while maintaining engaging, fast-paced gameplay.
